## We ship internationally — about $35 USD

Buttons Bebe ships internationally, including to **Israel**. The international
shipping option appears at checkout, costs approximately **$35 USD**, and delivery
times vary by destination. The customer is responsible for any customs fees or
import duties charged by their country (conservative default — confirm specifics
with owner).

## No prepaid international return labels

We **do not provide prepaid return labels for international returns**. If an
international customer needs to return an item, they ship it back **at their own
cost**, or have a **US-based family member or friend** send it on their behalf, or
drop it off / mail it from within the US when someone is there. See
`escalation-and-edge-cases.md` (international / US-address customers) and
`return-windows-and-refund-tiers.md`.

## Redirecting an in-transit international order

Once a package is in transit, the store cannot redirect it. Suggested options for
the customer: have a family member or neighbor at the US/forwarding address collect
it, or contact the carrier to hold it for pickup at the local office. Do not promise
a reship or refund — route service issues to a human.
