## Policy — shipping time depends on method

Customer wants shipping timeframe.

Shipping time depends on method selected. Processing time is separate.

## Shipping estimates

USPS: approximately 7–14 days
UPS: usually next day
ETA shipping: usually 1–2 days
Processing time: usually 24–48 hours before carrier transit begins

## Customer response

Hi! Shipping time depends on the method selected at checkout.
USPS can take approximately 7–14 days.
UPS is usually next day.
ETA shipping is usually around 1–2 days.
Please note that processing time is separate from shipping time. Orders usually ship within 24–48 hours before the carrier transit time begins.
