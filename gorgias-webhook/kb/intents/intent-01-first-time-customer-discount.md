## Policy — no first-time customer discount

Customer asks if there is a first-time customer discount.

Buttons Bebe does **not** offer a first-time customer discount.

## Agent action

Do not offer a code unless there is an active public promotion.

## Customer response

Hi! Thank you so much for reaching out and welcome to Buttons Bebe.
At this time, we do not offer a first-time customer discount. However, we do run sales and promotions from time to time, so the best way to stay updated is through our emails/texts and website.
Please let us know if you need help with anything while placing your order!
