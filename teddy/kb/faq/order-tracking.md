# Order Tracking and Status

## How to check tracking

A tracking number is emailed when the order ships (within 24–48 hours of ordering).
Check spam/junk folder if not received.
Customer can also log into their account and view order history.

---

## What status labels mean

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Processing / Unfulfilled** | Order received, being prepared (1–3 business days) |
| **Label Created** | Shipping label printed; carrier has not scanned it yet |
| **In Transit** | Package moving between carrier facilities |
| **Out for Delivery** | On the delivery truck today |
| **Delivered** | Package has been delivered |

---

## Tracking not updating

Common for 24–48 hours after label creation — carrier must physically scan the package.

If no updates after **5 business days** → contact support with order number to investigate with carrier.

---

## Package shows delivered but not received

Follow this flow in order:

1. **Ask the customer to check around first** — neighbors, building mailroom/office or concierge, package locker, front door, porch, back door, garage, mailbox, and other household members. Carriers sometimes mark a package delivered a little early, so waiting 24 hours can help too.
2. **If still not found → check whether package protection was purchased:**
   - **With package protection** → issue a full refund. See [Package Protection](../policies/package-protection.md).
   - **Without package protection** → the store cannot cover it directly. Help the customer **file a claim with the carrier**.
3. **If the carrier claim is denied or fails → escalate to a human.** Do not promise a refund on your own authority. Document the outcome in the ticket notes.

**Customer response (asking customer to check first):**
> Hi! We're sorry to hear that — the tracking shows it as delivered. Could you please check with any neighbors or your building office, and look in alternate spots like the front door, back door, porch, or garage? Carriers sometimes mark packages delivered a little early, so it may also turn up within a day. If it still hasn't shown up, let us know and we'll look into next steps with you.

## When will my order ship?

Orders ship within **24–48 hours** of purchase. See [Shipping Policy](../policies/shipping.md) for full timing details.
