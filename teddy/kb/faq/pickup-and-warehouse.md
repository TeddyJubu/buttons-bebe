# Pickup, Warehouse, and Drop-Off Locations

Buttons Bebe operates out of a **warehouse** (not a traditional retail store), but customers are welcome to visit during hours to browse, try things on, pick up orders, or drop off returns.

---

## Locations and hours

| Location | Address | Hours |
|---|---|---|
| **Warehouse — Toms River** | 2133 Lakewood Road, Unit 104, Toms River, NJ 08755 | Mon–Thu 9am–3pm · Fri 9am–2pm |
| **Lakewood return drop-off** | 6 Kenyon Drive, side door (return bin) | 24/7 |
| **Toms River pickup bin** | Outdoor bin at the warehouse address | 24/7 |

Extended warehouse hours are sometimes available — customers should confirm hours before visiting (see contacts below).

---

## Order pickup

When placing an order, the customer selects the Toms River pickup option at checkout. The order is placed in the **outdoor pickup bin at the warehouse, accessible 24/7**. Orders are held in the bin for approximately **2 months**.

**Customer response (pickup ready):**
> Hi! Your order is ready for pickup at 2133 Lakewood Road, Unit 104, Toms River, NJ 08755. It's in our outdoor pickup bin, which is accessible 24 hours a day, so you can come by anytime. Orders stay in the bin for about 2 months. If you have any trouble finding it, just let us know!

---

## Returns drop-off

Returns can be dropped off in person (no label needed) at either:
- **6 Kenyon Drive, side door, Lakewood** — 24/7 return bin
- **2133 Lakewood Road, Unit 104, Toms River, NJ 08755** — during warehouse hours

**Customer response (drop-off options):**
> Hi! You're welcome to drop off your return at our 24/7 return bin at 6 Kenyon Drive (side door) in Lakewood, or at our warehouse at 2133 Lakewood Road, Unit 104, Toms River, NJ 08755 during hours (Mon–Thu 9am–3pm, Fri 9am–2pm). No label needed for drop-off.

---

## Visiting to try items on

Customers are welcome to come to the warehouse during hours to try items before buying.

**Customer response (try-on visit):**
> Hi! You're welcome to stop by our warehouse to try things on: 2133 Lakewood Road, Unit 104, Toms River, NJ 08755 (Mon–Thu 9am–3pm, Fri 9am–2pm). We sometimes have extended hours, so feel free to confirm hours before your visit. See you soon!
