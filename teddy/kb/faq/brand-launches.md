# Brand and New Arrival Launch Dates

## Policy

- If the launch date is **known** → answer with the date.
- If the launch date is **not known** → give a general answer. Do not guess.
- If **many customers are asking** about the same brand and no date is saved → escalate to staff and save the answer for future use.

## Agent steps

1. Check saved launch calendar or internal notes.
2. If date is known → answer.
3. If date is not known → give general answer.
4. If volume of the same question is high → escalate to staff to confirm and save the date.

## Customer response if date is unknown

> Hi! New arrivals are posted regularly, and launches are usually announced through our website, emails/texts, or Instagram. We don't always have an exact time to share, but if a date is available, we're happy to let you know.

## Customer response if date is known

> Hi! [Brand] is expected to launch on [date/time]. We recommend checking the site and our emails/texts for updates.
