# Gift Wrapping

## Is gift wrapping available?

Yes! Gift wrapping is **complimentary (free) on full-price items valued at $35 or more**.
**Sale items are not eligible** for gift wrapping. Gift boxes can be purchased separately — confirm pricing with the team.

## How to add gift wrapping

Gift wrap **must be selected at checkout** by checking the gift wrap option. **A written note alone is not enough** — the packing team only sees the checkbox selection, not a note in the order comments.

At checkout you can also:
- Add a personalized gift message printed on a small card
- Request a gift receipt (no prices shown)

**Customer response (gift wrap policy):**
> Hi! We offer complimentary gift wrapping on full-price items valued at $35 or more. Sale items aren't eligible, but you're welcome to purchase a gift box separately. Just be sure to select the gift wrap option at checkout so our packing team sees it!

**Customer response (gift wrap wasn't checked at checkout):**
> Hi! We're so sorry about that. The gift wrap option has to be checked at checkout for our packing team to see it — a note in the order alone unfortunately doesn't reach them. We'll make a note for any future orders. So sorry for the inconvenience!

## Gift message

Your handwritten-style gift message is printed on a small card and included in the package.
The recipient will see your message when they open their gift.

## Gift receipt

A gift receipt (with no prices) is automatically included when you check the "This is a gift" box.
The recipient can use the gift receipt for exchanges.

## Can I add gift wrapping after ordering?

If you forgot to add gift wrapping, email us at **support@buttonsbebe.com** within **12 hours** of placing your order with your order number and gift message.
We will do our best to accommodate the request, though we cannot guarantee it if the order has already been packed.

## Delivery directly to recipient

You can ship directly to the gift recipient by entering their address as the shipping address.
Your billing address and order details will not be included in the package.

## Returns on gift-wrapped orders

Orders that include gift wrapping are eligible for **store credit only** — they cannot be refunded to the original payment method. See [Returns and Refunds](../policies/returns.md).
