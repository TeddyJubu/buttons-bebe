# Care Instructions

## General care (all items)

- **Machine wash cold** on a gentle/delicate cycle
- **Tumble dry low** or lay flat to dry — high heat can shrink fabric and fade colors
- **Do not bleach** — bleach damages the fabric and prints
- **Do not iron prints directly** — turn inside out or use a pressing cloth
- Wash darks separately for the first 1–2 washes to prevent color transfer

## Specific fabric care

**Cotton and cotton blends:**
- Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
- Tumble dry low
- May shrink slightly on first wash — size up if in doubt

**Fleece and knitwear:**
- Machine wash cold, gentle cycle
- Lay flat to dry (tumble drying can cause pilling)
- Do not wring or twist

**Items with embroidery or appliqué:**
- Turn inside out before washing to protect decorative elements
- Gentle cycle, cold water
- Lay flat to dry

## Tips for long life

- Zip up zippers before washing to prevent snags
- Empty all pockets
- Wash inside out for printed items to preserve colors
- For stubborn baby stains: pre-treat with a mild stain remover before washing

## What to avoid

- Hot water (causes shrinking and color fading)
- Dryer on high heat
- Bleach or chlorine-based products
- Dry cleaning (not necessary and damages some fabrics)
