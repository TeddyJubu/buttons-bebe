# Fabric and Material Information

## Core rule: Do not guess

**Never** guess or estimate fabric composition. Answer only if the material is confirmed from:
- Product page
- Product description
- Vendor data
- Saved internal notes or previous staff answer

If the information is not available → **escalate to a human**.

---

## When material is known

> Hi! This item is made of [fabric/material].

---

## When material is unknown — escalate

> Hi! We can try to check the fabric/material for you. We don't want to guess if it isn't listed, so we'll check and get back to you with the best information available.

---

## Common Buttons Bebe fabrics (for reference — always verify per product)

These are general examples only. Always confirm per-product before answering:

- 100% Organic Cotton — used in softest basics (onesies, bodysuits)
- Cotton/Polyester Blend (80/20) — used in tops, pants, rompers
- Fleece (100% Polyester) — used in hoodies and outerwear
- Rib Knit (95% Cotton, 5% Spandex) — used in cuffs and fitted styles

**Important:** These are category examples. Do not use these to answer a specific product question without confirming the actual product data.
