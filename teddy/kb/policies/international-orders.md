# International and Israel Orders

## International shipping

Buttons Bebe ships internationally, including to **Israel**. The international shipping option appears at checkout.

- International shipping is approximately **$35 USD**.
- Delivery times vary by destination.

**Customer response (do you ship internationally / to Israel):**
> Hi! Yes, we ship internationally, including to Israel. You'll see the shipping option at checkout — international shipping is approximately $35 USD, and delivery times vary depending on your location.

---

## International returns

**We do not provide prepaid return labels for international orders.** A customer who needs to return an item would either:
- Ship the item back to us at **their own cost**, or
- Have a **US-based family member or friend** send it back on their behalf (or use a US forwarding/shipping address).

When in the US, a customer can also drop a return at the warehouse or return bin in person — see [Pickup and Warehouse](../faq/pickup-and-warehouse.md).

**Customer response (international return):**
> Hi! For international orders, we're unfortunately not able to provide a prepaid return label. You're welcome to ship the item back to us at your own cost, or if it's easier, you can have a US-based family member or friend send it back on your behalf. Let us know and we'll share the return address.

---

## Redirecting an international package in transit

Once a package is in transit, we cannot redirect it. The customer can have a family member or neighbor collect it, or contact the carrier to hold it for pickup at their local office.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! Once a package is in transit we're unable to redirect it, but a couple of options can help: a family member or neighbor can collect it for you, or you can contact the carrier to ask them to hold it for pickup at your local office.
