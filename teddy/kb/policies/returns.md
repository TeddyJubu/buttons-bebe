# Return and Refund Policy

## Refund window

Orders are eligible for a **cash refund for 7 days after delivery**. **Day 1 = the day of delivery.** To qualify for a cash refund, the return package must be **scanned in by the shipping carrier within 7 days of delivery**.

After the 7-day window, eligible returns may only qualify for **store credit** instead of a cash refund.

Items that are 1–19% off, gift-wrapped, or baby gifts are **store credit only** even within the first 7 days — see the sections below and [Final Sale](final-sale.md).

**Baby gift return window:** Baby gifts can be returned for **store credit only**, within about **21 days of delivery** (mined practice — confirm the exact window with the team).

## How to tell a customer their refund deadline

1. Locate the order.
2. Check the delivery date (Day 1 = delivery day).
3. Calculate 7 days from delivery.
4. Tell the customer their exact deadline.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! Your order is eligible for a cash refund within 7 days of delivery (the delivery day counts as Day 1). To qualify, the return package needs to be scanned in by the shipping carrier within those 7 days. After that window, eligible returns may only qualify for store credit.

---

## Store credit instead of refund

If a customer received store credit and expected a cash refund:
- Check the delivery date.
- Check the return scan date.
- If the return was scanned **after the 7-day window** → store credit is correct per policy. Explain the timing.
- If the return was scanned **within 7 days** → the system may have auto-issued store credit in error. See **System auto-issued store credit** below to override it to a cash refund.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! Returns are eligible for a cash refund only within the first 7 days after delivery. After that window, returns convert to store credit instead. We can check your order and confirm the timing for you.

---

## System auto-issued store credit (override)

The return portal sometimes **automatically issues store credit (a gift card)** when a return is scanned in — including cases where the customer was actually still inside the 7-day cash-refund window.

**Agent steps:**
1. Verify the delivery date and the return scan date (Day 1 = delivery day).
2. If the return was scanned **within 7 days** → the customer is owed a cash refund. Override it: deactivate the auto-issued gift card, then issue a manual cash refund on the order.
3. If the return was scanned **after the 7-day window** → store credit is correct; do not override. Explain the timing.
4. If unsure, escalate to a human before reversing any store credit.

**Customer response (within 7 days — correcting to cash refund):**
> Hi! It looks like store credit was issued automatically, but your return was within the cash-refund window, so we've corrected that to a cash refund back to your original payment method. It can take a few business days to appear depending on your bank.

## Return conditions

Items must be:
- Unworn and unwashed
- With original tags attached
- In original condition

**Cannot be returned:**
- Items marked **Final Sale** at time of purchase
- Swimwear (hygiene)
- Gift cards
- Items returned after the refund/credit window

---

## Final sale items

Final sale items are not eligible for return or exchange by mail after shipping.
Any exception must be warehouse-approved only.
See [Final Sale Exception Policy](final-sale.md).

## Restocking fee

A small restocking fee is deducted from **cash refunds and cancellations** because Buttons Bebe is charged a processing fee by the credit card company on every refund.

| Scenario | Fee |
|---|---|
| Standard return (customer's choice) | **$1.69 per item** |
| Order cancellation | **$1.99 per item** |
| KIN KIN brand items | **$3.00 per item** |
| Store error (wrong item, damaged item) | **Waived** |
| Store credit instead of cash refund | **No fee** |

**Always offer store credit as the fee-free option** when a customer is unhappy about the fee — store credit is issued for the full amount with no deduction.

If a customer disputes the fee claiming it was a store error, verify in the order. If it was genuinely the store's mistake, waive the fee.

**Customer response (explaining the fee):**
> Hi! The small restocking fee covers the processing fee our credit card provider charges us on every refund, and it applies to returns and cancellations. If you'd prefer to avoid it, we're happy to issue your refund as store credit instead — that's the full amount with no deduction. Just let us know which you'd prefer!

**Customer response (waived — store error):**
> Hi! Since this was an error on our end, we've waived the restocking fee and processed your full refund. We're so sorry for the inconvenience!

## Gift-wrapped orders → store credit only

Orders that include gift wrapping are eligible for **store credit only** — they cannot be refunded to the original payment method.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! Since this order included gift wrapping, returns are eligible for store credit rather than a refund to the original payment method. We'd be happy to set that up for you.

## Return conditions — tags, washed, and worn items

Items must come back **clean, unworn, unwashed, with all original tags and accessories** (belts, bonnets, straps, etc.).

**Tags removed:**
- Ask the customer if they still have the tags. If yes, they can include them and we can re-attach them — the return can be accepted.
- If the tags are gone but the item otherwise looks new, use judgment; escalate for higher-value items.

**Washed items:**
- Washed items are **not** eligible for return or exchange.
- **Exception:** if a defect (peeling print, broken snap, stitching failure) only became visible after washing, that is a **manufacturer defect**, not a washing issue — it IS covered. See [Damaged or Wrong Items](../faq/damaged-items.md). When in doubt, escalate before promising a refund.

**Worn but not washed:**
- Use judgment based on condition. Hygiene items (swimwear, undergarments) are never returnable regardless.

**Customer response (tags removed):**
> Hi! No problem — do you still have the tags? If so, just include them in the return package and we'll be able to process it for you.

**Customer response (item washed):**
> Hi! We're sorry, but we're unable to accept a return on items that have been washed. If there's a defect with the item, please send us a photo and we'll do our best to help from there.

## Missing accessory — partial refund

When an item arrives missing an accessory that should have been included (sunglasses, hat, belt, straps):

1. Check whether we have a spare accessory. If yes → ship it separately, no return needed.
2. If no spare → offer the customer a choice: a **partial refund** for the missing piece, or a **full refund** with a return label for the whole item.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! We're sorry the [accessory] wasn't included. Unfortunately we don't have a spare available. Would you prefer a partial refund for the missing [accessory], or would you rather return the full item for a refund? Just let us know and we'll take care of it.

---

## Customer keeps the item (agent discretion)

For **low-value** items, the agent may tell the customer to keep an incorrect or damaged item rather than requiring a return — for example when a replacement has already shipped, or the item is defective and low value. Do **not** do this for high-value items without staff approval.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! No need to send it back — please keep it, it's on us. We've already processed your [refund/replacement]. Thank you for your patience!

---

## Combining returns from multiple orders

Customers may ship items from different orders back in the **same package**. Ask them to include a note inside identifying each item and its order number so the warehouse can process each return correctly. See drop-off options in [Pickup and Warehouse](../faq/pickup-and-warehouse.md).

**Customer response:**
> Hi! Yes, you can send items from different orders in the same package. Just include a quick note inside listing each item with its order number so our warehouse can match everything up correctly.
