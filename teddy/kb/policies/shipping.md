# Shipping Policy

## Processing time

Orders usually ship within **24–48 hours**.
This is the processing time before the package leaves Buttons Bebe — it is **separate from carrier delivery time**.

**During sale season,** order volume is very high and processing can take up to **4–5 business days**. Set this expectation with the customer warmly and thank them for their patience.

**Customer response (sale-season processing delay):**
> Hi! Thank you for reaching out. Due to the high volume of orders during our current sale, processing can take up to 4–5 business days before your order ships. We're getting it out as quickly as we can — thank you so much for your patience!

---

## Sale season — returns and exchanges

During an active sale season, the return and exchange rules change:
- **Mail returns are not accepted.**
- Exchanges are **in-warehouse (in person) only.**

See [Order Changes / Exchanges](exchanges.md) for the full sale-season exchange flow and warehouse details.

## Shipping method delivery estimates

| Method | Estimated Delivery |
|---|---|
| USPS | Approximately 7–14 days |
| UPS | Usually next day |
| ETA shipping | Usually 1–2 days |

**Important:** USPS can be slow. If a customer needs an order by a specific date, recommend a faster shipping option.

**Customer response for "how long will shipping take":**
> Hi! Shipping time depends on the method selected at checkout. USPS can take approximately 7–14 days. UPS is usually next day. ETA shipping is usually around 1–2 days. Please note that processing time is separate from shipping time — orders usually ship within 24–48 hours before carrier transit time begins.

## When will my order ship?

1. Locate the order.
2. Check fulfillment status.
3. If **unfulfilled**: explain 24–48 hour processing time and note that USPS delivery time is separate.
4. If **shipped**: provide tracking number and update.

**Customer response if unfulfilled:**
> Hi! Orders usually ship within 24–48 hours. Please note that this is the processing time before the package leaves us — carrier delivery time depends on the shipping method selected at checkout. USPS can be slow, so if you need an order by a specific date, we recommend choosing a faster shipping option.

---

## Urgent orders / customer needs order by a specific date

1. Locate the order.
2. Send message to warehouse to prioritize if possible.
3. Check selected shipping method.
4. If slow shipping was selected and order has not shipped, offer upgraded shipping option.
5. **Do not guarantee delivery** unless the shipping method and carrier support it.

**Customer response:**
> Hi! We'll do our best to help. We can send a message to the warehouse asking them to try to get your order out faster. Please note that delivery timing will still depend on the shipping method selected at checkout. If you need it by a specific date and the order has not shipped yet, we can check whether a faster shipping option is available.

---

## Free shipping threshold

Free standard shipping applies on orders over $75.
