PHASE 1  ·  LIVE TODAY
Where we are today
The agent already works as a copilot. For every incoming ticket it reads the message, pulls the order, return and product details, searches the knowledge base, and writes a first-draft reply — placed in Gorgias as a private note for your team to review and send.
The safety promise
The AI never sends on its own. Every customer reply is written by the AI but sent by a human. Sensitive tickets are flagged, never auto-handled.

APPENDIX  ·  FOR THE BUILD
Technical notes & estimate assumptions
Phase 3 — architecture notes
Auto-send = confidence score + classifier gate + per-intent switch + kill switch + full logging.
New channels ride Gorgias' native chat / social / SMS integrations where possible.
Actions (refunds, returns, discounts, order edits) = new gated writes to Shopify & Redo — today the only write is a Gorgias internal note.
Multi-language builds on the existing multilingual KB embeddings; add per-language routing.
Model evaluation: test a less verbose / cheaper model than glm-5.2 to cut cost & cleanup.
What the estimates assume
One focused builder (Tony + Claude), working sequentially.
Estimates are build + test time; ranges absorb testing & surprises.
Chaim's 5 policy answers and any channel/action logins arrive promptly.
Scope stays fixed per item; features can be re-ordered to taste.
Days = focused work-days, not calendar days; ~1 week ≈ 5 build-days.
