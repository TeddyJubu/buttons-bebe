## What size should I get for my child? (do not guess)

Sizing should **not** be guessed. Answer only if reliable fit info exists (product
page, product/vendor notes, saved previous staff answers). If reliable info is not
available, escalate to a human — don't invent measurements. Fit varies by brand and
style. See `../intents/intent-04-sizing-help-multiple-items.md`,
`../intents/intent-05-how-does-item-run.md`,
`../intents/intent-09-measurements.md`, and `../agent-core-rules.md` is the
authoritative do-not-guess rule (`../policies/agent-core-rules.md`).

## This item runs big/small — is that normal?

Do not guess how an item runs. Answer only from reliable product data or staff
history; if unknown, escalate. If two items that should match appear mis-sized
versus each other, that may be a manufacturing inconsistency — ask for a photo and
treat it as a possible defect rather than a buyer return. See
`../intents/intent-05-how-does-item-run.md`.

## How do I wash/care for an item?

Follow the garment's care label. General conservative advice for baby clothing
(state as guidance, not a guarantee for a specific item): wash cold on a gentle
cycle, avoid bleach on prints/colors, and dry low or lay flat since many baby
cottons and knits can shrink on high heat. If item-specific care is needed and we
don't have it, say we'll check rather than guessing.

## Do you gift wrap?

Gift wrapping is available for items at or above a minimum value — agents have
cited **$35 or more** per item. Sale items are generally **not** gift-wrapped, but
a customer can ask to pay for gift boxes. Confirm the current threshold before
quoting it.
