## General care guidance

[PLACEHOLDER — no official care data yet.] We carry many brands, so care varies
by item and fabric. The safe, conservative answer is: **follow the care label on
the garment.** Typical conservative defaults for baby clothing — only state these
as general advice, never as a guarantee for a specific item:
- Wash cold, gentle cycle; turn delicate or printed items inside out.
- Avoid bleach on colored or printed fabrics.
- Tumble dry low or lay flat to reduce shrinkage; many baby knits and cottons can
  shrink on high heat.

If the customer needs item-specific instructions we don't have, say we'll check
and route to a human rather than guessing.

## Shrinkage / damage after washing

If an item shrank, faded, or was damaged after washing per the label, this may be
a defect — gather details/photos and check with the vendor (see
`warranty-and-defects.md`). Do not promise a refund (escalation-only).
