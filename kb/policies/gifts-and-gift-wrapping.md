## Gift wrapping is free on full-price items $35+

Gift wrapping is **complimentary on full-price items valued at $35 or more**.
**Sale items are not eligible** for gift wrapping. Gift boxes can be purchased
separately (confirm pricing with the team). When a customer asks for gift wrap,
check that the order qualifies before confirming.

## Gift wrap must be checked at checkout

Gift wrap is only applied if the customer **checks the gift-wrap option at
checkout** — the packing team works from that flag, so a written note alone (in the
order notes or an email) is **not seen** by packing. If a customer wanted gift wrap
but didn't check the box, apologize, explain the checkout-checkbox requirement, and
flag it for any future orders. Before the order ships, a gift **note** can be added
to the order on request.

## Gift-wrapped orders are store credit only

An order that includes **gift wrapping** is eligible for **store credit only** — it
cannot be refunded to the original payment method. Treat this like the other
store-credit-only categories when handling a return. The store-credit money action
is escalation-only (see `refunds-and-disputes.md`); the agent may explain the
policy.

## Returning a gift — private, store credit, 21 days

A gift recipient can return a gift **without the sender being notified** — gift
returns are kept completely private. Baby gifts can be returned for **store credit
within 21 days of delivery**. Ask for the **invoice number** on the packing slip to
look up the original order, then arrange the return. See
`return-windows-and-refund-tiers.md`.
