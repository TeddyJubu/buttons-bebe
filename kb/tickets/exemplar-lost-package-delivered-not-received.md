## Customer situation

Tracking shows the order was delivered, but the customer says it never arrived.

## How it was handled

The agent first asked the customer to check the usual places (neighbors, household,
alternate drop spots) and looked for a carrier delivery photo. Only after that did
the agent branch on whether package protection was purchased — covered if yes,
carrier claim if no — without promising a refund directly, since refunds are
escalation-only.

## Ideal reply (first response)

> Hi — so sorry to hear that! Tracking shows it was delivered, so before anything
> else, could you please check with neighbors or your building office, any alternate
> spots (porch, garage, back door), and others in your household? If it still hasn't
> turned up after that, let me know and we'll look into next steps right away.

## Why this is the model

- Checks the most common resolution (misplaced/neighbor) before escalating.
- Sets up the package-protection vs. carrier-claim branch without over-promising.
- Does **not** promise a refund — see `../policies/lost-or-stolen-package.md`,
  `../policies/package-protection.md`, and `../policies/refunds-and-disputes.md`.
- Replace any signature placeholder with the agent's own name before sending.
