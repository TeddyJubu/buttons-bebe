## Policy — sizing advice for multiple items

Customer wants sizing advice for multiple items.

Sizing advice should not be guessed. The agent may answer only if reliable fit information exists.

## Agent action

Check product page.
Check product notes.
Check saved previous answers.
Check brand/vendor sizing notes.
If reliable info exists, answer.
If not, escalate to human.

## Customer response if info is not immediately available

Hi! We’d be happy to try to help with sizing.
Sizing can vary by brand and style, and we don’t always have exact sizing details for every item. We’ll check and do our best to guide you based on the information we have.
