## Policy — refundable for 7 days after delivery

Customer wants to know refund deadline.

Order is refundable for 7 days after delivery. Return must be scanned by the shipping carrier within 7 days of delivery.

## Agent action

Locate order.
Check delivery date.
Calculate 7-day refund window.
Tell customer deadline.

## Customer response

Hi! Your order is refundable for 7 days after delivery.
To qualify for a refund, the return package needs to be scanned in by the shipping carrier within 7 days of the order being delivered.
After that window, eligible returns may only qualify for store credit.
